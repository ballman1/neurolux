The Science Behind Neurolux Duo FL-41

Understanding how our specialized lenses work to provide relief

Precision Light Filtration

Neurolux Duo FL-41 lenses combine two scientifically validated light filtration technologies:

1. FL-41 Tint Spectrum

Specially formulated to block the narrow band of blue-green light (480-520nm) that has been clinically shown to trigger photophobia and migraines.

2. Blue Light Filter

Blocks harmful high-energy blue light (400-450nm) from digital devices and LED lighting that contributes to eye strain and sleep disruption.

This dual-action filtration provides comprehensive protection while maintaining excellent color perception and visual clarity.
